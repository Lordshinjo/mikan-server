Mikan Server

How to install?

  1. Download and install Prism Launcher.
  2. Launch Prism Launcher and sign in.
  3. Click on "Add Instance", then "Import", paste the following link: https://github.com/Lordshinjo/mikan-server/releases/download/latest/Mikan_Server.zip, then "Ok".

How to run?

  1. Launch Prism Launcher.
  2. Double click the "Mikan_Server" icon.
  3. That's it, it should automatically join the server!
    If it doesn't, just go into Multiplayer and try to join again.
